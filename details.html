Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to the celebration?

We recommend elegant casual attire. Think garden party chic - comfortable yet sophisticated. Since the event is outdoors at a glamping venue, comfortable shoes are essential. Layers are also recommended as the evening may get cool.

Is there a gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to contribute something special, we're collecting memories and photos for a commemorative album. You can also make a donation to Joy's favorite charity in her honor.

What if it rains?

The glamping tents are fully weatherproof and climate-controlled. The celebration will proceed rain or shine. In case of severe weather, we have indoor backup facilities available at the same venue.

Can I bring my children?

This is an adults-only celebration. We appreciate your understanding as we create an intimate evening for family and friends. We can recommend local childcare services if needed.

Is dietary accommodation available?

Yes! Please indicate any dietary restrictions or allergies in your RSVP. Our chef can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs with advance notice.
